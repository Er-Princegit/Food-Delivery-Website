The Heart Behind Foodie

Every great brand has a story — and Foodie began with a dream to make delicious, hygienic, and fast food delivery accessible to everyone.

Prince Kumar

Founder & CEO of Foodie

Prince Kumar started Foodie with a simple goal — to bring joy through food. With a background in technology and a passion for hospitality, he created a platform where convenience meets quality. Under his leadership, Foodie has become a trusted name for delicious and fast delivery service.

His vision is to empower local restaurants and provide customers with the easiest way to enjoy their favorite meals — anytime, anywhere.
